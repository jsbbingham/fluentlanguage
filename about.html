About Isela Bingham

Professional Spanish Interpreter & Document Translator

20+ Years of Professional Experience

Isela Bingham is a bilingual Spanish interpreter and document translator based in Stockton, California, with over 20 years of professional experience serving individuals, families, and organizations across legal, medical, and educational settings.

Professional Background

Since 2006, Isela has provided independent contracting services through Valley Mountain Regional Center, specializing in the translation of complex documents including medical reports, psychological evaluations, IFSPs, IPPs, and IEPs.

She has extensive experience conducting simultaneous interpretation for large-scale conferences of 100+ attendees, as well as consecutive and sight interpretation in face-to-face and virtual settings.

Professional Environments

Her background spans a broad range of professional environments — from supporting attorneys in workers' compensation law to serving as an Assistant Clients' Rights Advocate — giving her a deep command of legal and institutional terminology in both English and Spanish.

Current Contracting

She currently contracts with Reliable Translations, Inc. and Now Interpreters, accepting both remote and on-site assignments, including IEP meetings at schools throughout the Stockton area.
